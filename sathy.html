Biography of

Dr.M.Akila

     "A good education is a foundation for a better future" Holistic education reflects and responds more fully than conventional education. It is just need of the hour to revisit the education system. Here comes a selfless, impeccable, intuitive woman Ms. R.Sathy, Headmistress of the Panchayat Union Primary School in Malumichampatti near Coimbatore. She roped in L&T, which adopted the school, gave it a makeover and also constructed washrooms and drinking water units with reverse osmosis (RO). One of her landmark initiatives was the 'Kutti Commandos'(Little Commandos). She formed a group of Fifth Grade students to go around the locality early in the morning and check whether people are defecating in the open. Her vision is all that matters into transforming the face of education that caters to the demands of the society. Her talk would offer a great insight to all the educationalists who would like to bring a change in the system. It's a great pleasure for TEDxKPRIET to have you for the event.
