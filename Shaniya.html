Biography of

Shaniya

     "Communication is a piece of art and the one who masters in it is an artist who leads his way to success". We have one of the best student rhetorician of our institution. Shaniya .S, alumni of our KPRIET, is a bright relentless postulant and a proficient orator who aspires to be a civil servant. She had mastered several ceremonies within the institution and is the best at on the spot oration. Her fondness towards the language has resulted in an initiative helping students to learn to communicate better as a team. For the reason that communication is the most demanded soft skills, She shall share the 5 absolutely necessary skills for successful communication and the tactics to master them. The skillset includes listening, speaking, non-verbal communication, stress management and emotional control. Tackling these 5 skills assists in success as a person trying to explain his version of thoughts. She is here to share her ideas on improving communicative skills as a beginner and focus on tips to overcome the introvert that hides in every person. To communicate our thoughts in the best of ways is what we have a language for and developing the skill of communication is the way to succeed. TEDx KPRIET feels elated and pleasure to have her where the session shall definitely reach on to the audience with the most positive response as every individual desires to succeed.
