Biography of

Arun pandian

     "As more and more artificial intelligence is entering into the world, more and more emotional intelligence must enter into leadership" Mr.‌Arun Pandian, He is currently pursuing his undergraduate in Computer science and Engineering at KPRIET.He is currently working as a Data Scientist Intern at a leading AI Based Company. He is a foundry member of AI research and development team in college. As an undergraduate researcher he has also contributed to a number of Multi-disciplinary Research in collaboration with an abroad university. He researched and co -authored two research papers on computer vision in Structural engineering. He has done numerous AI Projects like COVID-19 Detector AI, Sentiment analyst and Digit recogniser and had also developed various Web applications . Apart from technical skills he has sharpened his Communication Skills by being a part of various clubs and competitions.He is currently working on Research paper in field of Applied Deep learning in Healthcare. He says that Artificial intelligence is helm of the technologies which are designing future. He will be boarding the stage of TEDxKPRIET to speak on Implications of Artificial Intelligence.
